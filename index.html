Title

Shinya: The Lunar Angel

Genre

Souls-like Action RPG / Story-driven Adventure

Description

Shinya: The Lunar Angel follows the story of Yuuka Shinya, a young girl battling against fate and family. Nojathra, the central realm, is plunged into eternal shadow, caused by Yuuka's mother. Throughout Yuuka's journey, she struggles through betrayal, heartbreak, and intense battle, whilst facing decisions putting the lives of her universe in jeopardy.
