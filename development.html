DEVELOPMENT

OVERVIEW

This section will go over the development process of Shinya, including its current status, design philosophy, and future plans.


PROJECT STATUS

Shinya is currently in active development. Core systems are being designed and implemented alongside narrative structure, worldbuilding, and early gameplay prototypes.

Development is iterative and subject to change as systems are refined and the scope is carefully managed.

The project began planning in early 2024, with pre-production focusing on concept development, narrative outlining, and technical research. However, due to school and other commitments, progress has been slow and gradual, with a focus on laying a solid foundation for future work.

DEVELOPMENT PHILOSOPHY

Shinya is built with a focus on atmosphere, emotional weight, and player agency. Design decisions prioritize tone and consequence over spectacle, ensuring that mechanics and narrative reinforce one another rather than exist in isolation.

The game favors deliberate pacing, meaningful encounters, and systems that support exploration and interpretation rather than direct exposition. The player should feel immersed in a living world that responds to their actions and choices.

SCOPE AND STRUCTURE

Shinya is structured as the first entry in a larger narrative. While the full story spans multiple acts and future installments, this project is designed to stand on its own with a complete arc and resolution.

Content is developed with long-term continuity in mind. Openings within the story offer potential for expansion and for players to theorize about the broader universe.

CURRENT FOCUS

Current development efforts are being focused on lapEngine, however other aspects such as narrative, worldbuilding, and art direction are also being explored in parallel.

Once I am able to move to full-production, gameplay systems, area design, and narrative integration will be developed during the prototyping phase.

ONGOING AND FUTURE WORK

As said before, gameplay systems, area design, and narrative integration will be the primary focus once full production begins. Alongside that, a main focus will be on refining a combat system that is engaging, challenging, and rewarding. Planned development includes expanded environments, additional enemy and boss encounters, deeper narrative branching, and refinement of player choice systems.

I am currently researching more into mesoamerican culture to see how that would fit within the world of Shinya as its major inspiration in style, tone, and lore. Most games like Shinya tend to draw from european medieval culture, so I want to explore something different while still keeping familiar elements that players would recognize.
